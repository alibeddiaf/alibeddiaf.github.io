Physically-Based Rendering of Particle-Based Fluids with Light Transport Effects

A. Beddiaf, M.C. Babahenini

3D Research, Springer, 2018

Back to Publications

Abstract

This paper presents a novel approach for physically-based rendering of particle-based fluids with realistic light transport effects. We introduce techniques for accurate simulation of light interaction with fluid particles, including refraction, reflection, and subsurface scattering. Our method achieves high-quality visual results while maintaining computational efficiency suitable for real-time applications.
